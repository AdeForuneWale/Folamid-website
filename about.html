Our Nigerian Culinary Story

From Lagos to the World - Celebrating Nigerian flavors while embracing international excellence in culinary education and event management

Our Journey Through Nigeria

A decade of celebrating Nigerian hospitality and building culinary excellence internationally

2014

Born in Lagos

Started with a passion for Nigerian cuisine in the heart of Lagos. Our first jollof rice catering service quickly became the talk of Victoria Island, serving authentic Nigerian flavors to local celebrations.

2017

Full Nigerian Event Experience

Expanded to offer complete traditional and contemporary Nigerian event management - from naming ceremonies to elaborate Nigerian weddings, bringing cultural authenticity to every celebration.

2020

Nigerian Culinary School Launch

Established our vocational training school focusing on Nigerian specialties alongside international techniques, addressing the skills gap in Nigeria's growing hospitality industry.

2024

West African Excellence Hub

Now serving 500+ Nigerian events annually and training 1000+ students internationally, proudly representing Nigerian hospitality standards worldwide.

Our Nigerian Culinary Programs

Authentic Nigerian cuisine training with international standards for global opportunities

6 Months

Professional Cooking

Nigerian Specialties: Jollof Rice mastery, Pepper soup, Suya preparation, Pounded yam & Egusi, Plus French, Italian & Asian techniques

Complete culinary foundation covering Nigerian classics and international cuisine, kitchen management, and food cost control. Learn to prepare authentic jollof rice that wins competitions!

4 Months

Pastry Arts & Baking

Local & International: Chin chin, Meat pie, Buns, Doughnuts, Plus French pastries, Wedding cakes, Chocolate work

Master both traditional Nigerian baked goods and international pastry techniques. From perfect chin chin to elaborate wedding cakes for diverse market opportunities.

3 Months

Food Service Management

Nigerian Business Focus: Local regulations, NAFDAC compliance, Nigerian customer service, Traditional event catering

Restaurant management, catering business setup, staff training, and financial planning adapted for the Nigerian market and regulatory environment.

Ongoing

Event Management and Decorations

Nigerian Event Expertise: Traditional weddings, naming ceremonies, corporate events, festival catering

Comprehensive training in managing Nigerian cultural events, understanding traditional requirements, modern event trends, decoration techniques, and client management in the Nigerian context.
