Culinary Excellence, Global Impact

For over a decade, Folamid Catering and Event Planning Limited has been celebrating Nigeria's rich culinary heritage while embracing international flavors. We're passionate about showcasing the diversity and excellence of African cuisine to the world.

From Lagos to London, our team has catered events that honor Nigerian traditions while incorporating global culinary trends. Our vocational school continues to train the next generation of African chefs and hospitality professionals.
